About Us

In our ever growing virtual world, entertainment in the world wide web has surely taken its own place among the society. Not only has it taken the world by storm but has brought unity to another level. Players from across the globe are able to access and interact with one another as if they were in the same room. An amazing achievement to mankind.

However, we would like to take a step into the future, crossing the bridge of reality and virtuality. Making the impossible, the predictable. We, here, at IIUM eSports Gaming Base would aim to host every tournament, anywhere across the world. Reuniting talented players and gifted individuals to challenge each other and produce the best among themselves, showing the world what they can do.

Now, with our recent embarkment on a breathtaking journey. Let us get you up to speed for what had began during the year 2018 with the provided video.

Before we are able to achieve this, history is important. Hence, let's take a step back into time to understand, view and learn all the lessons that has taken humanity to where they are now. IIUM eSports Gaming have been developed in 2018 by one of the most promising team in IIUM, we believe that greatness can be achieved together. Therefore, we would like to build a platform where gamers from around the world are able to find themselves among the many people who can call this website, home.

During our progress to develop this website, there were many obstacle our team had overcome. From being released from the University of our beloved foundation to facing bankruptcy. Nonetheless, our resilience kept our hopes up and with sponsorship such as Nestle and Intel. They saw our true potential. Thus, a great platform was soon built at the Kulliyah of Information Technology in IIUM. Preseverence and dedication helped us to achieve our goal.
